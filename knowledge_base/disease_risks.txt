GREENHOUSE DISEASE RISK GUIDE — TOMATO
========================================

FUSARIUM WILT:
- Trigger conditions: Temperature above 28 degrees AND Humidity above 80 percent
- Risk level: HIGH when both conditions persist for more than 2 hours
- Prevention: Maintain humidity below 75 percent, ensure good air circulation
- Action: Increase ventilation, reduce humidity immediately

BOTRYTIS GRAY MOLD:
- Trigger conditions: Temperature below 20 degrees AND Humidity above 85 percent
- Risk level: VERY HIGH in cool humid conditions
- Prevention: Maintain temperature above 18 degrees, humidity below 80 percent
- Action: Increase heating, maximum ventilation

POWDERY MILDEW:
- Trigger conditions: Low light AND Humidity above 70 percent AND Temperature 20 to 25 degrees
- Risk level: HIGH in shaded humid conditions
- Prevention: Ensure adequate light above 300 lux, good air movement
- Action: Increase supplemental lighting, improve ventilation

PYTHIUM ROOT ROT:
- Trigger conditions: Soil moisture above 85 percent for extended period
- Risk level: HIGH when soil waterlogged more than 4 hours
- Prevention: Never allow soil moisture above 80 percent
- Action: Stop all irrigation immediately, improve drainage

HEAT STRESS:
- Trigger conditions: Temperature above 35 degrees for more than 30 minutes
- Effects: Flower abortion, fruit cracking, reduced yield
- Prevention: Never allow temperature above 32 degrees during flowering
- Action: Emergency cooling protocol immediately

WATER STRESS:
- Trigger conditions: Soil moisture below 30 percent for more than 2 hours
- Effects: Blossom end rot, fruit cracking, stunted growth
- Prevention: Maintain soil moisture above 40 percent
- Action: Immediate irrigation

LIGHT DEFICIENCY:
- Trigger conditions: Light below 150 lux during 8am to 6pm
- Effects: Etiolation, poor fruit set, weak stems
- Action: Activate grow lights at full capacity
