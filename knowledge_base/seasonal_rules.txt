SEASONAL AND TIME GUIDELINES FOR GREENHOUSE MANAGEMENT
=======================================================

SUMMER JUNE TO AUGUST:
- Temperature management: HIGHEST PRIORITY check ventilation every 30 minutes
- Ventilation: Run ventilation proactively before temperature exceeds 30 degrees
- Irrigation: Increase to 3-4 times daily
- Lighting: Supplemental only needed on cloudy days
- Energy: Focus on cooling, heating not needed

SPRING MARCH TO MAY:
- Temperature management: Ventilate during afternoon 12pm to 4pm
- Humidity: Monitor closely, spring humidity can be high
- Irrigation: Increase frequency as temperature rises
- Priority: Ventilation and temperature balance

AUTUMN SEPTEMBER TO NOVEMBER:
- Temperature management: Balance heating and ventilation
- Humidity: Increasing risk, monitor for disease
- Irrigation: Decrease frequency as temperature drops
- Lighting: Supplemental lighting increasingly needed
- Priority: Disease prevention and heating efficiency

WINTER DECEMBER TO FEBRUARY:
- Temperature management: Heating is PRIMARY concern
- Ventilation: Minimize, only ventilate when CO2 is critical
- Irrigation: Reduce to 1-2 times daily
- Lighting: Maximum supplemental lighting needed
- Priority: Heating over ventilation always

TIME OF DAY GUIDELINES:
- Night 10pm to 6am: No irrigation, minimal ventilation, heating priority
- Early morning 6am to 9am: Begin irrigation, check overnight conditions
- Morning 9am to 12pm: CO2 enrichment, optimize light
- Afternoon 12pm to 4pm: Peak temperature management, maximum ventilation if needed
- Evening 4pm to 8pm: Reduce irrigation, prepare for night settings

ENERGY OPTIMIZATION RULES:
- Never run heating and ventilation simultaneously at maximum
- Use supplemental lighting only when natural light below 200 lux
- Schedule irrigation during morning to reduce night humidity
- Ventilation priority order: CO2 flush then temperature then humidity
- Heating priority: Temperature maintenance over humidity control
