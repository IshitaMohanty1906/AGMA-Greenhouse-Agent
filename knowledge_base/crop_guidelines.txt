GREENHOUSE CROP GUIDELINES — CHERRY TOMATO
==========================================

TEMPERATURE GUIDELINES:
- Optimal daytime temperature: 20-25 degrees Celsius
- Optimal nighttime temperature: 16-18 degrees Celsius
- Maximum safe temperature: 32 degrees Celsius
- Critical high temperature: above 35 degrees causes flower drop and fruit damage
- Emergency temperature: above 40 degrees causes plant death within hours
- Minimum safe temperature: 15 degrees Celsius
- Critical low temperature: below 10 degrees causes chilling injury

HUMIDITY GUIDELINES:
- Optimal relative humidity: 60-75 percent
- Acceptable range: 50-80 percent
- High humidity warning: above 80 percent increases disease risk
- Critical high humidity: above 85 percent causes fungal disease outbreak
- Low humidity warning: below 50 percent causes water stress and tip burn
- Critical low humidity: below 40 percent causes severe wilting

CO2 CONCENTRATION GUIDELINES:
- Ambient CO2 level: 400 ppm
- Optimal CO2 for photosynthesis: 800-1200 ppm
- CO2 enrichment recommended: 800-1000 ppm during daylight
- High CO2 warning: above 1200 ppm with poor ventilation
- Critical CO2: above 1500 ppm requires immediate ventilation
- Emergency CO2: above 2000 ppm is toxic to plants and workers

LIGHT INTENSITY GUIDELINES:
- Minimum light for photosynthesis: 150 lux
- Optimal light intensity: 400-700 lux
- Maximum beneficial light: 800 lux
- Supplemental lighting needed: when natural light below 200 lux during 6am to 8pm
- Night period: no supplemental lighting needed after 10pm
- Daily light integral: minimum 12 mol per square meter per day for tomatoes

SOIL MOISTURE GUIDELINES:
- Optimal soil moisture: 50-70 percent
- Acceptable range: 40-75 percent
- Irrigation trigger: below 35 percent soil moisture
- Critical low moisture: below 25 percent causes immediate wilting
- Waterlogged warning: above 85 percent causes root rot
- Critical high moisture: above 90 percent causes anaerobic conditions

NUTRIENT AND WATER GUIDELINES:
- Irrigation frequency: 2-4 times daily depending on growth stage
- Avoid irrigation after 6pm to reduce night humidity
- Morning irrigation preferred: 7am to 10am optimal window
