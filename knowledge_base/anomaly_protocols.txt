GREENHOUSE ANOMALY ACTION PROTOCOLS
=====================================

TEMPERATURE ANOMALY PROTOCOLS:

Protocol T1 - High Temperature Warning 28 to 35 degrees:
- Action: Activate ventilation at 50 percent capacity
- Action: Check if heating system is accidentally ON
- Alert level: WARNING

Protocol T2 - High Temperature Critical 35 to 40 degrees:
- Action: Activate ventilation at 100 percent capacity immediately
- Action: Pause all heating systems immediately
- Action: Send alert to operator
- Alert level: CRITICAL

Protocol T3 - High Temperature Emergency above 40 degrees:
- Action: Maximum ventilation immediately
- Action: Activate all cooling systems
- Action: Send emergency alert immediately
- Alert level: EMERGENCY

Protocol T4 - Low Temperature Warning 15 to 18 degrees:
- Action: Activate heating system at 30 percent capacity
- Action: Close ventilation partially
- Alert level: WARNING

Protocol T5 - Low Temperature Critical below 15 degrees:
- Action: Activate heating system at 100 percent
- Action: Close all ventilation
- Action: Send alert to operator
- Alert level: CRITICAL

HUMIDITY ANOMALY PROTOCOLS:

Protocol H1 - High Humidity Warning 80 to 85 percent:
- Action: Increase ventilation by 30 percent
- Action: Pause irrigation temporarily
- Action: Monitor for disease signs
- Alert level: WARNING

Protocol H2 - High Humidity Critical above 85 percent:
- Action: Maximum ventilation activation
- Action: Stop all irrigation immediately
- Action: Send alert for fungal disease risk
- Alert level: CRITICAL

Protocol H3 - Low Humidity Warning below 50 percent:
- Action: Reduce ventilation rate
- Action: Check irrigation schedule
- Alert level: WARNING

CO2 ANOMALY PROTOCOLS:

Protocol C1 - High CO2 Warning 1200 to 1500 ppm:
- Action: Increase ventilation rate by 40 percent
- Action: Check ventilation system operation
- Alert level: WARNING

Protocol C2 - High CO2 Critical above 1500 ppm:
- Action: Maximum ventilation flush run for 15 minutes
- Action: Check for CO2 enrichment system malfunction
- Action: Send alert to operator
- Alert level: CRITICAL

SOIL MOISTURE ANOMALY PROTOCOLS:

Protocol S1 - Low Moisture Warning 25 to 35 percent:
- Action: Activate irrigation immediately
- Alert level: WARNING

Protocol S2 - Low Moisture Critical below 25 percent:
- Action: Emergency irrigation full capacity
- Action: Send alert plant wilting imminent
- Alert level: CRITICAL

Protocol S3 - High Moisture Warning above 85 percent:
- Action: Stop all irrigation
- Alert level: WARNING

COMPOUND CONDITION PROTOCOLS:

Protocol CC1 - High Temperature plus High Humidity:
- Action: Ventilate at maximum addresses both conditions
- Action: Stop irrigation completely
- Action: Send fusarium disease risk alert
- Alert level: CRITICAL
- Important: Do NOT irrigate when humidity is already critical

Protocol CC2 - High Temperature plus Low Moisture:
- Action: Ventilate to reduce temperature
- Action: Irrigate at moderate rate to cool roots
- Alert level: CRITICAL

Protocol CC3 - Low Temperature plus High Humidity:
- Action: Activate heating
- Action: Increase ventilation slowly
- Action: Alert for botrytis disease risk
- Alert level: CRITICAL
